Introducing SceneGen

Our software is a mockup generator designed for dynamic, market-specific IKEA advertising, leveraging AI-generated imagery to create seasonally relevant and modifiable mockups. The innovation lies in its ability to rapidly produce high-quality visual content tailored to various geographic markets and input factors, drastically reducing production time.

Working With The Three Design Thinking Principles

Understanding the Customer

Our innovation centers around the creation of AI-generated, market-specific imagery for IKEA's marketing and product development purposes. This led us to design a tool that allows users to dynamically select options such as season, time of day, materials, style, and market to create personalized, highly relevant imagery that resonates with their target audience. By putting the user's needs first, we ensure that the generated images are not only visually appealing but also culturally meaningful.

Generating Concepts

During the ideation phase, we explored various creative approaches to help IKEA generate high-quality images that capture different atmospheres and emotions. From surveys with employees at IKEA we saw that there was a need for a tool which could aid in generating general design and scene ideas. By leveraging AI-powered tools like DALL-E, we enable IKEA teams to visualize potential new products or marketing campaigns with minimal effort at a lower time & cost basis. This flexibility ensures that the tool remains adaptable to new markets and trends, enhancing IKEA's ability to stay ahead of customer expectations.

Prototyping and Testing

Our focus has been on refining the prototype internally, ensuring that the tool is user-friendly and capable of producing high-quality visuals that meet IKEA's design standards as well as possible. Although formal user feedback is still as of now pending , we have ensured that the system is flexible, allowing for continuous improvements based on future testing and real-world use.
