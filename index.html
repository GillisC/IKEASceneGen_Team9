IKEA SceneGen

Introducing IKEA SceneGen

Our software is a mockup generator designed for dynamic, market-specific IKEA advertising, leveraging AI-generated imagery to create seasonally relevant and modifiable mockups. The innovation lies in its ability to rapidly produce high-quality visual content tailored to various geographic markets and input factors, drastically reducing production time.

Demo Illustration

Working With The Three Design Thinking Principles

Empathy: Understanding the Customer's Needs

Our innovation centers around the creation of AI-generated, market-specific imagery for IKEA's marketing and product development purposes. At the heart of this project is a deep understanding of IKEA's diverse customer base, particularly in the Scandinavian and European markets. Through extensive research, we have identified that customers from these regions value design that reflects local seasons, cultural nuances, and specific lifestyle preferences. This insight led us to design a tool that allows users to dynamically select options such as season, time of day, materials, style, and market to create personalized, highly relevant imagery that resonates with their target audience. By putting the user's needs first, we ensure that the generated images are not only visually appealing but also culturally meaningful.

Ideation: Generating Innovative Concepts

During the ideation phase, we explored various creative approaches to help IKEA generate high-quality images that capture different atmospheres and emotions. The concept of allowing marketers to toggle multiple attributes—such as style, season, and feeling—was born out of brainstorming sessions focused on how we could streamline IKEA's creative process. By leveraging AI-powered tools like DALL-E, we enable endless combinations of aesthetic elements, empowering IKEA teams to visualize new products or marketing campaigns with minimal effort. This flexibility ensures that the tool remains adaptable to new markets and trends, enhancing IKEA's ability to stay ahead of customer expectations.

Prototyping and Testing

We iteratively tested and refined our prototype by gathering feedback from potential users, including IKEA designers and marketing professionals. Each iteration brought us closer to a solution that is both user-friendly and effective in generating impactful visuals. We incorporated feedback on the interface's usability, ensuring that it allows for seamless selection of attributes like materials and audience without overwhelming the user. Additionally, the AI-generated images were compared against IKEA's existing visual standards, ensuring they meet the company’s high expectations for creativity and quality. This testing process is crucial as it allows us to continuously improve and deliver a tool that adds real value to IKEA's operations.
